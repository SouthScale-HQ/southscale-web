About SouthScale

A small, independent software company building consumer products from the UK.

SouthScale is in the early stages of building. We're not backed by venture capital, we don't have a large team, and we're not trying to move fast and break things. We're focused on building one product at a time and making it genuinely good.

BYN is our first product — a play-money sports prediction market that we've been quietly building and testing with real users. It's live now at bynapp.online.
